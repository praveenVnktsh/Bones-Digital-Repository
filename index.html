What is this project?

This project developed at the Archaeological Sciences Center at IIT Gandhinagar is meant to be a large scale digital access repository of artifacts and specimen obtained through Archaeological surveys conducted at the institute.

Who is this for?

This project is meant to allow open access to the data collected by the Archaeological Sciences Center at various sites. We hope that this material will be useful for students and researchers around the globe.
